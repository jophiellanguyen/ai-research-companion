# AI Research Companion

## Overview

AI Research Companion is an AI-powered research assistant designed to support researchers throughout the scientific discovery process.

The system helps researchers explore literature, identify research opportunities, organize knowledge, develop hypotheses, and accelerate research workflows.

Instead of replacing researchers, AI Research Companion acts as an intelligent collaborator that enhances human creativity, reasoning, and scientific exploration.

---

## The Problem

Scientific research is becoming increasingly complex.

Researchers face challenges including:

- Rapid growth of academic publications
- Difficulty discovering relevant knowledge
- Time-consuming literature review processes
- Identifying meaningful research gaps
- Organizing information across multiple sources

Traditional research workflows require significant manual effort to connect ideas and build new insights.
